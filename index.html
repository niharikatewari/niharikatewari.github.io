I'm a cotutelle Ph.D. researcher in the School of Computing at RMIT University and the Department of Biological Sciences and Department Computer Science and Information Systems at BITS Pilani, K.K. Birla Goa Campus, under the supervision of Prof. Feng Xia, Prof. Ziqi Xu and Prof. Veeky Baths (BITS Pilani). My research focuses on graph machine learning, explainable artificial intelligence (XAI), and neuroimaging for the early diagnosis and progression analysis of Alzheimer's disease and related dementias using multimodal brain imaging data.

My research interests span graph neural networks, explainable artificial intelligence (XAI), multimodal learning, and computational neuroimaging. I aim to develop machine learning methods that are theoretically grounded, interpretable, and practically useful for advancing healthcare and biomedical applications.
