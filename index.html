About

🎓 Expert in Explainable AI for Graph Neural Networks (XAI-GNN) & Dementia Research
I am a passionate Doctoral Researcher specializing in Explainable AI for Graph Neural Networks (XAI-GNN) and its applications in dementia research. Currently working on an exciting collaborative project between BITS Pilani and RMIT University, I focus on harnessing AI and graph analytics for the early detection of neurodegenerative diseases.

🧒 Data Science & AI for Healthcare
With a strong foundation in Data Science from my tenure as an Assistant Professor, I stay at the cutting edge of emerging trends in AI and graph-based healthcare analytics. As an Advisory Board Member for conferences like ICMESS, I contribute to advancing the field and promoting innovations in neurotechnology and AI healthcare.

🧠 Open to Collaboration
Ask me about Biomedical Engineering, Computer Vision, Machine Learning, and Deep Learning. I am always open to new collaborations and opportunities to innovate in the field of Artificial Intelligence and healthcare applications.
